## üÜò Troubleshooting

### **Skill Not Responding:**
1. **Check Internet:** Ensure your Echo Dot has internet connectivity
2. **Correct Invocation:** Make sure to say "ask bea pumpkin pi" not just "bea pumpkin pi"
3. **Clear Speech:** Speak clearly and at normal volume
4. **Try Again:** If it doesn't understand, rephrase your command

### **Audio Enhancement Not Working:**
1. **Device Limitations:** Remember this enhances processing, not hardware output
2. **External Speakers:** For best results, connect high-quality speakers to your Echo Dot
3. **Environment:** Ensure your Echo Dot is in an optimal acoustic environment
4. **Check Status:** Ask for a performance report to verify system operation

### **Beatbox Recognition Issues:**
1. **Clear Environment:** Minimize background noise for best recognition
2. **Microphone Distance:** Speak/beatbox at normal conversation distance
3. **Pattern Clarity:** Use distinct beatbox sounds for better recognition
4. **Try Different Styles:** The system recognizes various beatbox styles

### **Common Solutions:**
- **Restart Echo Dot:** Unplug for 10 seconds, then plug back in
- **Check Alexa App:** Ensure the skill is enabled in your Alexa app
- **Account Sync:** Verify you're using the same Amazon account
- **Update Device:** Make sure your Echo Dot has latest firmware
